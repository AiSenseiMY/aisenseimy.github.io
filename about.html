Our Story

From Passion for AI to a Mission for Impact

AiSenseiMY was born out of a shared belief that artificial intelligence should not be confined to tech giants and research labs. Founded in Kuala Lumpur, we set out to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI technology and the businesses that need it most.

What started as a small team of AI enthusiasts running weekend workshops has grown into a full-service AI consulting, training, and creative media company serving clients across Malaysia and beyond.

Our mission is to democratize artificial intelligence — making it accessible, practical, and transformative for businesses of all sizes. We believe that when people understand AI, they can shape a better future.
