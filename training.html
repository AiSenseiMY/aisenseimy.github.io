Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our AI training programs.

Who are the AI training programs designed for?

Our training programs are designed for professionals at all levels — from business leaders and managers who want to understand AI strategy, to developers and engineers who want hands-on experience building AI solutions. No prior AI experience is required for our foundation-level programs.

Can the training be customized for my organization?

Absolutely. Our corporate training programs are fully customizable. We work with your team to understand your industry, goals, and skill gaps, then design a tailored curriculum with relevant case studies and hands-on exercises specific to your business.

What tools and technologies are covered in the training?

Our programs cover a wide range of AI tools and frameworks including Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn, OpenAI APIs, Hugging Face, and popular cloud AI platforms. The specific tools depend on the program level and your team's needs.

Do participants receive a certificate after completing the training?

Yes, all participants receive a certificate of completion from AiSenseiMY upon finishing the program. Our certificates are recognized by industry partners and can be shared on professional profiles like LinkedIn.
