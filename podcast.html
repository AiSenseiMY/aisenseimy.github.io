About the Podcast

Conversations at the Frontier of AI

The AiSenseiMY Podcast brings you weekly conversations with AI researchers, business leaders, and creative technologists who are shaping the future of artificial intelligence. Whether you are a beginner exploring the AI landscape or a seasoned professional, our episodes offer practical insights, thought-provoking discussions, and actionable takeaways.

From deep dives into machine learning algorithms to lively debates about AI ethics and policy, we cover the full spectrum of AI topics. Hosted by the AiSenseiMY team, each episode is designed to inform, inspire, and empower.
