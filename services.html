Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our AI consulting services.

What is AI consulting and how can it help my business?

AI consulting helps businesses identify opportunities where artificial intelligence can drive efficiency, reduce costs, and create new revenue streams. Our consultants assess your current processes, data infrastructure, and business goals to create a tailored AI strategy and implementation roadmap.

How long does a typical AI consulting engagement take?

The duration varies based on scope. An AI readiness assessment typically takes 2-4 weeks, while a full strategy and implementation engagement can range from 2 to 6 months. We work in agile sprints to deliver value incrementally throughout the project.

Do I need a large dataset to benefit from AI solutions?

Not necessarily. While larger datasets can improve model accuracy, many AI solutions can work with modest data. We help you assess your existing data, identify gaps, and leverage techniques like transfer learning and data augmentation to maximize results even with limited data.

What industries do you serve with AI consulting?

We serve a wide range of industries including retail, finance, healthcare, manufacturing, education, and media. Our consultants bring cross-industry experience and adapt AI best practices to your specific sector's needs and regulatory requirements.
